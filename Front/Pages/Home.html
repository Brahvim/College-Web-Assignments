History:

The College was started in 2005 in a smaller location in The Town.

In 2008, it was moved to the outskirts of The Town, along with an infrastructural upgrade.

Many minor developments have been made since then.









Campus:

The College's campus has four buildings. These house:

  • Four computer labs,

  • And one each of a:
    • Physics,
    • Chemistry,
    • Electronics, and
    • Language laboratories.


  • A classroom with special tables
    and stools for the purpose of
    engineering drawing, named the
    "Drawing Hall".


  • Two toilets separated according
    to gender are available on each
    floor of these buildings.
